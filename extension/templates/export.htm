Export Settings

If you are signed in to Chrome, your settings are automatically backed up and sychronised with your Google account, however it's a good idea to keep a backup. This can be useful if you ever need to restore your settings or want to use Deadbolt on a version of Chrome that you don't sign in to (e.g. a work computer).

Download your settings to a file by pressing 'Export Settings' button below, and keep the file stored somewhere safe.
